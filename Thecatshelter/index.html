Our Mission

Our mission is to give loving homes to the best feline friends a person could ever ask for. We reallocate abandoned, rescued and homeless cats in all shapes and sizes. You can brows Our Cats section to find your new feline companion.From an ethical standpoint we encourage people to adopt and not shop as there are already 100s of cats in need ot loving homes. They're all clean, healthy and excited to meet you.
